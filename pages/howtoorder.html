How To Order

1. Login the website.

  1. Click on "login" which will lead you to Login.
  2. If you don't already have an account you can register from the same page where you will see a "register" text that will lead u to Register.
  3. When you are ready to order you may choose a category from our menu.
  4. If you would like u can see the ingredients of the dish u can click on "Options" which will lead you to a page showing the items ingredients.
  5. After you are done choosing you can press "Add To Cart" fill in the information and your ourder will be delivered to you!

2. Phone Orders

  1. Complete the steps from the preveous text.
  2. View our Menu and choose ur dish.
  3. Call us at: +359 4574 647 991.
  4. Provide your order details and delivery address.
  5. Our team will confirm your order and give you an estimated delivery or pickup time.

3. In-Restaurant Ordering

We welcome you to visit us! You can order directly at the counter or with your table server.

Payment Methods

  • Credit/Debit Card (Visa, MasterCard, Maestro)
  • Online payment through our website (secure gateway)
